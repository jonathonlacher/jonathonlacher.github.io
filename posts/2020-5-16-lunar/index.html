Sync your laptop and external monitor brightness

May 16, 2020

Since working from home, I’ve been using an external monitor more heavily. The light in my office changes throughout the day, and I found myself adjusting the brightness on my MacBook and external monitor separately.

I started looking for a utility that would link the brightness of the MBP and my external monitor, and I found Lunar.

In sync mode, the brightness of the laptop and external monitor are linked, which means I only need to adjust one brightness level for both.

It’s neat, you should try it.
